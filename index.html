Welcome to the Altus Institute of Higher Education

In order to clear competitive exams like UGC NET/ JRF, you need an excellent learning environment, that is conducive to imparting higher education in a planned and systematic manner. You need to be competent enough to become the frontrunner in this field. Altus promises you that space and support and gives wings to your aspirations.

Being one of the best NET coaching centres in Kerala, we ensure that you have rich learning experiences. We have UGC NET coaching centres in Calicut, Thrissur, Kochi and Manjeri.We offer you NTA and JRF NET online classes that utilize highly innovative teaching methodologies. UGC NET coaching in Commerce, English, Management, History and Economics subjects are available for those interested in them.
